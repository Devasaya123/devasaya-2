Sustainability & Values

A quieter kind of luxury

We believe the most radical thing a brand can do today is slow down.

Natural Dyes Only

Indigo, madder root, pomegranate rind, turmeric, iron filings — every pigment we use comes from the earth and returns to it.

Water-Conscious

Wastewater is filtered through reed beds before it returns to the soil. No chemical effluent leaves our workshop.

Fair Wages

Our artisans set their own pace and price. We pay above the regional average and ensure year-round work.

Made to Outlast

An Ajrakh saree, well kept, will outlive its owner. Slow fashion is a refusal of waste.

"We do not chase trends — we tend to a tradition."
